Meet Your Attorney

A St. Louis lawyer who actually picks up the phone.

Anthony W. Sabat — Founder, Sabat Law Firm, LLC

Born and raised in the greater St. Louis area, Anthony's lifelong dream was to become a lawyer. Inspired by his Uncle John's legal career, he earned his degree from Saint Louis University School of Law and, in April 2024, fulfilled another dream by opening a firm of his own.

He started Sabat Law to practice the way he always believed law should be practiced — heartfelt, honest, and genuinely accessible. That means fair, transparent pricing, quick replies to your calls and texts, and an attorney who breaks down "lawyer language" so you always understand what's happening and why.
